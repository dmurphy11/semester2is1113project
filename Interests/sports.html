Sports

Favourite Sports:Hurling & Gaelic Football

Club:Barryroe GAA Club

Training:Tuesday, Friday & Sunday

Grade:Intermediate Hurling, Junior A Football

Age:U21 & Adult

Barryroe GAA is a small rural club, founded in 1892, which competes in the carbery (West Cork) division. Hurling would be stroger than football in Barryroe, with women's camogie and ladies football teams also present. Due to a lack of numbers for recent underage teams, Barryroe has formed an amalgamation with nearby club Argideen Rangers. This has allowed young players to play at a higher grade, thus improving themselves, benefiting their respective clubs when they return to play at adult level. Barryroe have always played in a sky blue and navy kit, and are well known as "The Blues".
